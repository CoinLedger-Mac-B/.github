## 💡 Overview

<a href="#coinLedger">CoinLedger</a> is a cryptocurrency tax calculation and portfolio tracking platform that automates the complex process of calculating taxable gains and losses from cryptocurrency transactions — connecting to exchanges, wallets, and DeFi protocols to import transaction history and produce accurate tax reports.

The <a href="#coinLedger">automatic transaction import</a> connects directly to major cryptocurrency exchanges and wallets through API connections and wallet address scanning — importing complete transaction histories from Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, and hundreds of other exchanges alongside self-custody wallets tracked by public address. Manual CSV import handles exchanges without direct integration. The automatic import eliminates the manually intensive process of tracking down every transaction across multiple platforms for tax calculation. The <a href="#coinLedger">capital gains calculation engine</a> applies the cost basis accounting methods that tax authorities accept — FIFO (first in, first out), LIFO (last in, first out), HIFO (highest in, first out), and specific identification — to every cryptocurrency transaction, calculating the cost basis and gain or loss for each taxable event automatically across thousands of transactions.

The <a href="#coinLedger">DeFi and NFT transaction support</a> handles the complex transaction types that decentralized finance and NFT activity generates — liquidity pool deposits and withdrawals, yield farming rewards, NFT purchases and sales, wrapped token conversions, and other DeFi interactions that standard exchange-focused tax tools often mishandle or ignore. The <a href="#coinLedger">tax-loss harvesting identification</a> highlights unrealized losses in the portfolio that could offset realized gains — identifying opportunities to sell positions at a loss before year-end to reduce the tax liability from gains taken earlier in the year.

<a href="#coinLedger">IRS Form 8949 generation</a> produces the specific tax form that cryptocurrency capital gains reporting requires — a complete, accurate Form 8949 with every taxable transaction listed, ready for filing or for providing to a tax professional. <a href="#coinLedger">TurboTax and tax software export</a> produces files formatted for direct import into TurboTax, H&R Block, and other tax preparation software — the cryptocurrency data flows into the tax return without manual re-entry.

<a href="#coinLedger">Portfolio performance tracking</a> provides a complete view of cryptocurrency holdings, historical values, and performance metrics — total portfolio value, gain/loss by asset, and historical performance charted over time. <a href="#coinLedger">International tax support</a> handles cryptocurrency tax reporting requirements for jurisdictions beyond the US — UK, Canada, Australia, and other countries with cryptocurrency tax obligations.
